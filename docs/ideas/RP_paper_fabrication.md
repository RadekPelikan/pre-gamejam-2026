### Lore

Create a game about agents which fabricate documents for public relations. Those agents publish documents about foreign terroristic subjects/countries/individuals with believable lies. Those papers are distributed to publishers and they spread around the world.

I take heavy inspiration from papers please for game lore and style. [Papers please gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kxPKnDLR0rA&list=PLa2jLRLGuaM10kbv9-INqXLVSwKehQLRF)


I'd like to keep the entire lore and names fictional. Do not include any real names of parties, secret agencies or politicians.

### Gameplay

I'm not sure about the gameplay yet. You could either play as an agent or some public investigator verifying your news from these agents. It could also be multiplayer for player competing in teams or indiduals in these two sides. There could be introduced another party. I'm not strict on the investigator part. But'd like to stay focused around the fabricated papers part.

The gameplay always needs some goal. The goal is the either continue in your fabricated propaganda or as the other party to reveal the lies of those agents. I'd also include some short lived goals of needing to care for your family or your own health, physical and mental.

If there would be the side of an investigator you could have to take other investigations in order to make money. Or do other things to keep yo living and able to go further on the agents journey.

So far I like the public investigator part more. Of player being the investigator. Multiplayer might suck the soul out of this. I'd like this to be lore heavy.
